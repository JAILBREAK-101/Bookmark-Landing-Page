Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of our FAQs. If you have any other questions you’d like answered please feel free to email us.

  • What is Bookmark?

    Bookmark is a software on the browser, that allows you to save webpages on the browser, to be accessible, by you later when you need it.

  • How can I request a new browser?

    You can request a new browser by downloading the browser that you desire to use, and then setting it as your default browser, for saving webpages on.

  • Is there a mobile app?

    Yes, there's a mobile application, and you can download it on the Play Store, or the App Store depending on your OS, and also based on the compatibilty of your device with the application.

  • What about other Chromium browsers?

    Other Chromium browsers have no problem working with Bookmark, for we work with Google.
